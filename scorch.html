The File

How He Got Here
⚠ Classified ⚠ Character Backstory — Spoilers — Click to Open

Scorch grew up in a shitty household. His dad died on an oil rig — an explosion — when Scorch was just a kid. His mom, Miranda, blamed him for it. Took it out on her only kid in every way a mother can. Emotional. Physical. Monstrous.

Skye was Scorch's best friend growing up. His brother in everything but blood. Scorch spent every hour he could over at Skye's place — and Donna, Skye's mom, always opened the door. Together, Skye and Scorch started The Snake Skins.

Then came the drugs. Scorch got addicted. Skye tried to help — for a while. And then, when nothing else worked, Skye thought turning him over to the police might save his life. Maybe it did.

Scorch served two years — he broke probation — and prison didn't humble him. It made him meaner. He lost all visitation rights to Conner. Skye never once showed up. Not one visit. Not one letter.

Now Scorch hates Skye. Hates the whole Street Rats lineup Skye whored himself to. Hates a lot of things. But he's still here. Still climbing back. Still trying to be in his kid's life.

He's got layers. Find them.
